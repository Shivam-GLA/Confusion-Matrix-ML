Errors in Machine Learning

Reduciable ErrorsReduciable Error in Machine learning means the mistakes that can be fixed or reduced by improving out model and the way we work with data.
These errors happen because our model might be too simple, too complex, or not trained well enough.

Irreduciable ErrorsIrreduciable Error is a kind of mistakes that will always be there,no matter how our good our model is.
This is because there is always some randomness or unpredictability in real-world data. And our model cannot eliminat it entirely.
